How Rankings work

  • Players earn ranking points based on finishing position and tournament size.
  • Your best 10 results from the past 12 months count towards your ranking.
  • Larger tournaments with more players award more ranking points.
  • Rankings update within 48 hours after each OPC-verified tournament.
  • Players must be verified and registered with OPC to appear in rankings.
