Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

OPC Europe ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you register or interact with our platform:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Email address and phone number
  • Nationality and country of residence
  • Age verification documentation
  • Tournament participation history and results

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Player verification and age confirmation
  • Calculating and displaying rankings
  • Communicating tournament updates and OPC news
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We share your ranking data (name, nationality, and points) publicly on our rankings page. We may share your verification status with OPC-certified tournament organizers to facilitate entry. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Ranking history is retained indefinitely as part of our public records. You may request deletion of your account data at any time.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and obtain a copy
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please visit our contact page.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and basic analytics. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. By using our website, you consent to the use of essential cookies.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, please visit our contact page.
