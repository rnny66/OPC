Luxon Pay

Luxon Pay is a fully FCA-regulated multi-currency eWallet that enables users to deposit, withdraw, exchange, and transfer money globally. Supporting over 30 currencies and available in more than 100 countries, Luxon Pay has become the go-to payment solution for poker players and live tournament participants around the world.

As official payment partner of OPC Europe, Luxon Pay makes it easy for players to buy into tournaments, manage their bankroll across currencies, and receive payouts directly into their wallet. Their Luxon Pay Mastercard enables contactless payments and ATM withdrawals wherever you play.

With zero fees on transfers, instant currency exchange, and advanced security backed by FCA regulation, Luxon Pay ensures our players can focus on what matters most — the game.
