About This Tournament

Amsterdam Open

The Amsterdam Open is one of the flagship events on the OPC Europe circuit, bringing together players from across the continent for four days of competitive poker at the iconic Holland Casino in Amsterdam.

As a free-entry tournament, the Amsterdam Open is the perfect opportunity for both seasoned players and newcomers to earn OPC ranking points, compete for prizes, and experience the thrill of live tournament poker in one of Europe's most vibrant cities.

Players will enjoy professional tournament structures, experienced dealers, and the world-class hospitality that Holland Casino is known for. Whether you're chasing the leaderboard or playing your first OPC event, the Amsterdam Open welcomes you.
